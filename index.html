  • Visualization for PymoNNtorch

    I am currently engaged in the development of a 3D real-time visualization and graphical interface for PymoNNtorch. PymoNNtorch is a spiking neural network system capable of harnessing the power of GPU!.

  • Real-time Visualization of 2D Tenseor

    I have leveraged the power of both OpenGL and CUDA, exploring their interoperability, to directly render tensors from the GPU. Through this approach, I achieved real-time visualization of PyTorch 2D tensors on CUDA devices without the need to transfer data to the CPU. This seamless integration between OpenGL and CUDA enables immediate representation of any changes made to the tensor within the render loop. The result is a dynamic and interactive visualization that brings the tensor's intricacies to life.
